## Agent-Specific Considerations (Clarification)

For clarification work, pay special attention to:
- **Hidden assumptions** in the user's request that may not be stated
- **Ambiguous terms** that could have multiple interpretations
- **Missing context** that would change the clarification approach
- **Success criteria** that define when clarification is complete
